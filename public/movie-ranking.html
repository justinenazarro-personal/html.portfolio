The Best Movies According to Justine

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

There Will Be Blood

A great movie that is shot well, has a great score, fantastic acting, and directed by one of my favorite diretors.

In Bruges

A darkly comic crime film about two hitmen who are forced to stay in a Belgian city after a botched job.

Clueless

A classic teen comedy that is both hilarious and heartwarming, following the life of a wealthy high school student navigating the ups and downs of adolescence.
